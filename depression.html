no great works


just a migraine
and a coffee
sitting on the couch
with the heater on

a record’s playing
but I’m not listening
books have piled up unread
these days the internet
gives the limbic system
all it needs

i’m alone
in a whole house
cobwebs building
in the corners of the ceiling
the broccoli lost
to weeds in the backyard

what’s the point?
the body says
sleeping in late again
the supermarkets are filled with food
just cook the same breakfast
make a plan about a list

another day lost
in preparation of a future
that stays a future
