welcome


life is holy
wholly lonely

just wanting
to take anchor in
another body

though only
temporary death of longing
when someone
somehow

pulls you from the fog
and tells you
welcome
welcome to my body

isn’t it funny?
how sometimes we
decide to touch each other
beneath the armor

everything slows
and everything that is you
is there and…

holy
wholly lonely

just wanting
to take anchor in
another body

though only
temporary all that
divine material is just
blood and organ

so for that
physical longing
just grab anyone
and tell them

I love you
